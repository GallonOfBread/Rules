Players: 3-20
Time: 3-20 mins
Ages: 6+

CONTENTS

216 cards as follows:
cards - 2x 0, 4x 1-9, Draw 2, Reverse, & Skip
cards - 2x 0, 4x 1-9, Draw 2, Reverse, & Skip
cards - 2x 0, 4x 1-9, Draw 2, Reverse, & Skip
cards - 2x 0, 4x 1-9, Draw 2, Reverse, & Skip
Wild cards - 8x Classic, & Draw 4

GOAL

To be the first player to run out of cards, who then can sign the card they won on.

SETUP

Deal each player seven cards. The remaining cards become the DRAW pile. Turn over the top card of the DRAW pile to start the DISCARD pile. If the first card of the DISCARD pile is not a number, see its effect under ACTION CARDS.

PLAY

In the first round, the player left of the dealer starts. On further rounds, the player who won then starts.

On your turn, you must match a card from your hand to the card on the top of the DISCARD pile by color, number, or symbol (symbols denote ACTION CARDS). If you cannot play a card, you can take one card from another player per turn. If no one is willing or you receive an unusable card, you must draw cards until you can play. You cannot accept a card from a player once you start drawing cards.

If it is not your turn, you may offer any card to the current player. It does not need to be playable, but trustworthy players get their cards taken more often. You may not give away your last card.

If you have a card of the same color AND the same number or symbol, you may play that card immediately. Play then continues from you. If you could play out-of-turn and someone is already drawing cards, you must ask them permission to play. If granted, they can then stop drawing cards.

ACTION CARDS

Card Action Action if First Action if Stacked
SEVEN Slap the table! The slowest player draws one card. Slap the table! The slowest player draws one card. Slap the table! Someone draws a card for every SEVEN played.
DRAW 2 The next player may stack a DRAW 2, or draw two cards for every DRAW 2 played. Stack a DRAW 2 or begin the game by drawing two cards. Add two cards to the count for the next player. When someone draws, the count resets.
REVERSE Reverse direction. Reverse direction and skip the first player. Every REVERSE reverses direction, even if it is confusing.
SKIP Skip the next player. Skip the first player. Stacked SKIPs only skip one neighboring player.
WILD Pick a color. Pick a color and play a card. Pick a color. WILDs can only be played out-of-turn onto like WILDs.
DRAW 4 Pick a color. The next player may stack a DRAW 4, or draw four cards for every DRAW 4 played. Stack a DRAW 4 or begin play by drawing four cards. Pick a color. Add four cards to the count for the next player. When someone draws, the count resets.
FROM THE SEWERS Pick a color. The next player may stack a FROM THE SEWERS, or draw cards until they have at least one card of each color, for every FROM THE SEWERS played, in their hand. Show everyone those cards to confirm. Stack a FROM THE SEWERS or begin play by drawing cards until they have at least one card of each color. Pick a color. Add one card of each color to the count for the next player. When someone draws, the count resets.

ENDGAME

When you play your next-to-last card, you must yell “UNO” to indicate that you have only one card left. A player draws two cards if they do not declare “UNO” and another player catches them by saying “TE PELLÉ”.

Once a player has no cards left, the round ends, and they sign the card they just played.
